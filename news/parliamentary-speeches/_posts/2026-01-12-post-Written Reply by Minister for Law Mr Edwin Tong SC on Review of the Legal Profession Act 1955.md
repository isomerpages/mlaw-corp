<b><u>Name and Constituency of Member of Parliament</u></b><br>
<b>Ms Hany Soh (Member for Parliament for Marsiling–Yew Tee GRC)</b>

<b><u>Question</u></b><br>
To ask the Minister for Law in view of the resolutions passed at 
the recent Law Society’s extraordinary general meeting (EGM) on 22 December 2025, whether the Ministry considers any amendments to the Legal Profession Act 1966 necessary.

<b><u>Written Answer:</u></b><br>

{:start="1"}
1.&nbsp;The Legal Profession Act 1966 (LPA) provides for 
the establishment of the Law Society of Singapore, including the composition of its Council and the election of office‑bearers.

{:start="2"}
2.&nbsp;We have noted the recent EGM. The Ministry will give due consideration to the points made at the EGM, as well as at other fora, as part of its regular review of the LPA to ensure its continued relevance and application.
