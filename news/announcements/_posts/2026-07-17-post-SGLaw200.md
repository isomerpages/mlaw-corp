<p>The Ministry of Law advances access to justice, the rule of law, the economy
and society through policy, law and services.</p>
<p>This year, we mark SGLaw200 – 200 years of Singapore’s modern judicial
and legal system, which traces its roots to the enactment of the Second
Charter of Justice in 1826. Our legal system and the Rule of Law have been
fundamental to Singapore’s development. They drive our economic growth,
ensure public safety, maintain racial and religious harmony, and support
our key social policies. As we commemorate the Bicentennial, we also take
the opportunity to look ahead, to explore how we can continue to evolve
the Rule of Law to meet our emerging challenges.</p>
<p>SGLaw200 reiterates our commitment to the Rule of Law where everyone is
equally subject to the laws and its protections. A trusted legal system,
a trusted Singapore.</p>
