<h3><strong>Responses to Feedback Received from Public Consultation on Proposed Legislative Amendments to DRS</strong></h3>
<p><strong>Consultation Period</strong>
<br>9 June 2025 to 27 June 2025</p>
<ol data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>The Ministry of Law would like to thank all respondents who provided their
feedback in the public consultation on the proposed legislative amendments
to the DRS, which took place from 9 June 2025 to 27 June 2025. We received
feedback from a wide range of stakeholders, including creditors such as
the financial institutions and licensed moneylenders, debtors and social
service agencies.</p>
<p></p>
</li>
<li>
<p>We have carefully considered all the feedback received and will incorporate
them, where applicable, in the upcoming Insolvency, Restructuring and Dissolution
(Amendment) Bill.</p>
<p></p>
</li>
<li>
<p>The Ministry of Law's detailed responses to the feedback concerning key
substantive policy matters can be found in the Annex here.</p>
</li>
</ol>
