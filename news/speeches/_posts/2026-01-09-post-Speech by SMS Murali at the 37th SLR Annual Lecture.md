Senior Minister of State for Law and Transport, Mr Murali Pillai SC, announced the launch of SGLaw200 at the 37th Singapore Law Review Annual Lecture on 9 January. In his lecture, SMS Murali also examined how Singapore developed its distinctive approach to the Rule of Law and how it must evolve to meet future challenges.
