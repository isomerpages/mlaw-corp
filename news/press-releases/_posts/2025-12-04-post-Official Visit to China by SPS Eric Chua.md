4 December 2025 Posted in [Press releases](/news/press-releases)

{:start=“1”}
1.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Senior Parliamentary Secretary of Ministry of Law and Ministry of Social and Family Development Eric Chua made an official visit to Haikou, China from 2 to 3 December 2025.

{:start=“2”}
2.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; SPS Chua attended the 9th International Mediation Summit organised by the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade (CCPIT) Commercial Legal Service Centre on 3 December. SPS also met with Hainan government officials and CCPIT leaders.

{:start=“3”}
3.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; At the 9th International Mediation Summit, SPS Chua delivered opening remarks, where he spoke about the importance of international dispute resolution and Singapore’s commitment to strengthening legal cooperation with China. A transcript of SPS Chua’s speech at the 9th International Mediation Summit is available at the following link: [https://www.mlaw.gov.sg/speech-by-sps-at-9th-ims/](https://www.mlaw.gov.sg/speech-by-sps-at-9th-ims/).

{:start=“4”}
4.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; SPS Chua was accompanied by officials from the Ministry of Law.
