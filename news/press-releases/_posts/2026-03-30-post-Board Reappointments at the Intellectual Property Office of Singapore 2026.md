1.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; The Ministry of Law (MinLaw) today announced the reappointment of board members at the Intellectual Property Office of Singapore (IPOS). These appointments will take effect from 1 April 2026.

**Reappointment of Board Members**

{:start=“2”}
2.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; The following have been reappointed to the IPOS Board for a term of two years.

<p style="margin-left: 40px">·&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Ms Usha Chandradas, Adjunct Associate Professor, NTU and Co-Founder, (Plu)ral Art LLP</p>

<p style="margin-left: 40px">·&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Mr Edgar Hardless, Chief Executive Officer, Singtel Innov8</p>

<p style="margin-left: 40px">·&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Prof Susanna Leong, President Advisor, NUS Chongqing Research Institute and Vice Provost (Masters’ Programmes &amp; Lifelong Education), National University of Singapore</p>

<p style="margin-left: 40px">·&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Mr Lim Der Shing, Co-Founder and Partner, AngelCentral Pte Ltd</p>

<p style="margin-left: 40px">·&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Dr Lim Jui, Chief Executive Officer, Singapore Innovate Pte Ltd</p>

<p style="margin-left: 40px">·&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Ms Lorraine Tay, Head of Intellectual Property Group, Bird &amp; Bird ATMD LLP</p>

<p style="margin-left: 40px">·&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Ms Winnie Tham, Co-Founder and Director, Amica Law LLC</p>

<p style="margin-left: 40px">·&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Ms Charlene Chang, Deputy Secretary (Development), Ministry of Law <i>(in an ex officio capacity)</i></p>
