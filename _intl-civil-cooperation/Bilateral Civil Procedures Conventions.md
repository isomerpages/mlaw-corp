<p>This provides online copies of the bilateral Civil Procedure Conventions
which Singapore is party to. This list may not be exhaustive, for the most
up-to-date list, please refer to the <a href="https://sso.agc.gov.sg/SL/scja1969-s914-2021?ProvIds=P12-PO70-Sc3-#P12-PO70-Sc3" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">Third Schedule of the Rules of Court 2021</a>.</p>
<p>1.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; The Convention Between the United Kingdom
and Germany Regarding Legal Proceedings in Civil and Commercial Matters,
signed in London on 20 March 1928 (continued in force between the Federal
Republic of Germany and the Republic of Singapore) <strong>[Link]</strong>
</p>
<p>2.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; The Convention Between the United Kingdom
and Italy Regarding Legal Proceedings in Civil and Commercial Matters,
signed in London on 17 December 1930 (continued in force between the Republic
of Italy and the Republic of Singapore) <strong>[Link]</strong>
</p>
<p>3.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; The Convention Between the Republic of
Austria and the United Kingdom Regarding Legal Proceedings in Civil and
Commercial Matters, signed in London on 31 March 1931 (continued in force
between the Republic of Austria and the Republic of Singapore) <strong>[Link]</strong>
</p>
<p>4.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Treaty on Judicial Assistance in Civil
and Commercial Matters Between the Republic of Singapore and the People's
Republic of China, signed in Beijing on 28 April 1997. <strong>[Link]</strong>
</p>
