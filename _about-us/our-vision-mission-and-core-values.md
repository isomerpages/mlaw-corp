<p><strong>Vision</strong>
</p>
<p>A Trusted Legal System; A Trusted Singapore.
<br>
<br>
</p>
<p><strong>Mission</strong>
</p>
<p>Advancing access to justice, the rule of law, the economy and society
through policy, law and services.
<br>
<br>
</p>
<p><strong>Our Core Values</strong>
</p>
<p><strong>Integrity</strong>
<br><em>We act with honesty and impartiality.</em>
<br><em>We take responsibility for our decisions and actions.</em>
<br><em>We do the right thing, in the right way.</em>
<br>
</p>
<p><strong>Service</strong>
<br><em>We treat everyone with respect and courtesy.</em>
<br><em>We seek to understand the needs of the people we serve.</em>
<br><em>We are ready to go the extra mile.</em>
<br>
</p>
<p><strong>Excellence</strong>
<br><em>We take pride in what we do and deliver quality and timely work.</em>
<br><em>We work together for the best results, and to bring out the best in one another.</em>
<br><em>We learn and innovate to do things better.</em>
</p>
